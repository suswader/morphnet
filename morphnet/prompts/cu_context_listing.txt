## Listing Page

This page has many items. To extract information:
- Use 'note' to record items as you see them (name, price, rating, etc.).
- Scroll down to reveal more items if needed.
- If scrolling reveals no new content after 2 attempts, all items are loaded.
- Do NOT click into individual items unless you need detail not visible on the listing.
- The AXTree already shows item names, prices, and ratings on listing cards.
